The Architect Hestia

Third Person Shooter / Survival / Adventure

The player finds themselves in a dystopian world where extreme wealth and poverty have torn society. Players play as Elian Cross, the protagonist who is amongst the poor Rusthaveners and becomes a symbol of hope and change in this divided world. The game is divided into two interconnected societies â€” Auravale and Rusthaven in which the player assumes a pivotal role in each society every time they die. In Rusthaven Elian leads a rebellion against the oppressive wealthy elite with minimal resources stealth and wit. In Auravale Elian becomes part of the rich, facing the consequences of the actions made from the wealthy while fighting off the rebellion with an abundance of resources. The two narratives are linked and Elian develops relationships with characters on both sides, where all actions in one society translate to the other.

Role and responsibility

All mechanics were built by me with no external libraries.

Gameplay Programming:

- Implemented player mechanics and animations for movement, such as walk, run, and crouch.
- Engineered the camera system to dynamically interact with the game environment, managing collisions with various in-game objects and terrain.

Enemy AI and Behavior:

- Created enemy AI systems, including tracking and decision-making processes.
- Implemented enemy states like "idle," "pursue," and "attack" for dynamic interactions between the player and AI-controlled entities.
- Programmed limb-specific damage mechanics, allowing for targeted attacks and diverse combat scenarios.

Weapon Mechanics and User Interface:

- Added a reload feature for players to easily manage ammunition, providing a straightforward yet crucial mechanic to enhance gameplay depth and combat strategy.
- Developed UI elements showcasing the equipped gun and real-time updates on available ammunition, enhancing player immersion and tactical awareness during engagements.
